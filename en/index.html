  • privacy policy

    How secure are the transactions with my credit card through the online system?

    As some people hesitate to do business with their credit card on the Internet, we can assure you that we have no access to details of your card (as the code of your credit card). When you trade with your credit card online through our system, the details of your card is not transferred to us but to the bank with which we work. Therefore, we have access to your bank account or your other credit card details.
    addition, when you want to finalize a booking on your system via your credit card, here is a link to the SSL-Server (Secure Socket Layer-Server) to do the transaction yourself. This server accepts the details of your card and transfer it to a secure site. The elements of your card then converted into coded bits are transferred on the Internet safely, and nobody can read them.

    How secure is my personal information?

    All personal information is strictly confidential, according to Greek law. We have the right to distribute or sell your personal information to others.
